How to get a bonus

Place multiple bets of at least 4 events (minimum odds 1.35 per event) with a minimum bet of €0.50 in the Sports or Live sections, and get up to 15% extra winnings according to the FAVBOOSTER grid.

Does not apply to System bets, Risk-Free bets, or CashOut.

What bets are included in the promotion?

Only Multiple-type bets (pre-match, live, or mixed) placed in the Sport and/or Live sections are considered.

What are the requirements for bets?

The grid applies to all tickets that include a minimum of 4 events, each with a minimum odds of 1.35.

Example:
If a ticket contains 10 events, of which 7 have minimum odds of 1.35 and 3 have odds below 1.35, the ticket is considered eligible for the 7 events that meet the eligibility criteria.

According to the Multi-Express grid, an additional winning percentage of 8% (equivalent odds: 1.08) will be applied.

The grid does not apply to System Bets or Risk-Free Bets, and in cases where the Cash Out option is used, it becomes invalid.
